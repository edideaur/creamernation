[ ABOUT ]

Creamer Nation is an experimental hip-hop collective formed in late 2023 by a group of close internet friends. Blending humor with emotion, their sound evolved from meme rap into something more intentional and expressive. The core members are Archivist, Whisp, MC KV!, Alis, and InfiniteCris. Their discography includes THE CREAMER NATION TAPE VOL. 1, MORE CREAM, CREAMER SEASON, A CREAMER NATION CHRISTMAS, and their latest EP, Three-Of-A-Kind.
